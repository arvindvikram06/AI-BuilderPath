# NeuraStack Technologies — Employee Handbook

## Company Overview
NeuraStack Technologies is an AI-powered SaaS company founded in 2020, headquartered in Austin, Texas. We build intelligent automation tools for engineering teams worldwide. Our mission is to help developers ship faster and smarter using AI.

---

## Work Hours & Remote Policy
- Standard work hours are 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM in the employee's local timezone.
- NeuraStack operates on a fully remote-first model. Employees may work from any location with a stable internet connection.
- Employees are expected to be available for a minimum 4-hour overlap with the Austin, Texas timezone (CST) each day.
- In-person team retreats are held twice per year (January and July) in Austin.

---

## Leave Policy

### Vacation Leave
- Full-time employees receive **20 paid vacation days** per year.
- Vacation days accrue at a rate of 1.67 days per month.
- A maximum of 10 unused vacation days may be carried over to the next calendar year.
- Vacation requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance through the HR portal.

### Sick Leave
- Full-time employees receive **10 paid sick days** per year.
- Sick days do not carry over and reset on January 1st each year.
- No advance notice required for sick days; notify your team lead by 10:00 AM on the day of absence.

### Public Holidays
- NeuraStack observes **12 public holidays** per year, including New Year's Day, Independence Day, Thanksgiving (US), and Christmas.
- Employees in non-US regions receive equivalent local public holidays.

### Parental Leave
- Primary caregivers receive **16 weeks** of fully paid parental leave.
- Secondary caregivers receive **4 weeks** of fully paid parental leave.
- Parental leave may be taken at any point within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.

### Bereavement Leave
- Employees receive **5 days** of paid bereavement leave for immediate family members (spouse, parent, sibling, child).
- **3 days** for extended family (grandparent, aunt, uncle).

---

## Benefits

### Health Insurance
- NeuraStack covers **100% of health insurance premiums** for employees and **80%** for dependents.
- Dental and vision coverage is included at no additional cost.
- Mental health support: employees receive 12 free therapy sessions per year through BetterHelp.

### Retirement (401k)
- NeuraStack matches **4% of the employee's salary** in 401k contributions.
- Vesting period is 2 years with a cliff vesting schedule.

### Learning & Development
- Each employee receives a **$2,000 annual learning budget** for courses, books, and conferences.
- Employees may take up to **3 paid study days** per year for certification exams.

### Equipment
- New employees receive a **MacBook Pro (M3, 16GB RAM)** and a $500 home office stipend.
- Equipment is refreshed every **3 years**.

### Other Perks
- Internet stipend: **$75/month**.
- Co-working space allowance: **$200/month**.
- Annual wellness stipend: **$500** (gym, fitness apps, meditation).

## Onboarding Process
- Week 1: Company orientation, tool setup, meet your team.
- Week 2: Product training and shadow sessions with senior engineers.
- Week 3: First assigned tasks with mentorship support.
- 30-day check-in with HR and team lead.
- 90-day performance and culture alignment review.

---

## Code of Conduct
- Treat all colleagues with respect regardless of role, location, or background.
- Harassment of any kind (verbal, written, or physical) will result in immediate termination.
- Conflicts should be reported to HR at hr@neurastack.io.
- Employees must not share confidential company information on social media or public forums.

---

## Performance Reviews
- Formal reviews occur **twice per year**: in June and December.
- Performance is rated on a 5-point scale across 4 dimensions: Impact, Collaboration, Growth, and Ownership.
- Salary reviews are tied to the December cycle.
- Employees rated 4 or above are eligible for promotion consideration.

---

## Termination Policy
- Voluntary resignation requires **2 weeks written notice**.
- Involuntary termination includes **2 weeks severance pay** for employees with less than 2 years of tenure, and **1 month per year of service** for longer-tenured employees.
